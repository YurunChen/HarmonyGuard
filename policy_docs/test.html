Company Security Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2024

  • Section 1
  • Section 2

1. Password Policy

All users must follow these password requirements:

  • Passwords must be at least 8 characters long
  • Passwords must contain at least one uppercase letter
  • Passwords must contain at least one number
  • Passwords must be changed every 90 days

2. Data Access Policy

Users are prohibited from:

  • Sharing their login credentials with others
  • Accessing data they are not authorized to view
  • Downloading sensitive information to personal devices
  • Using company resources for personal purposes

3. Acceptable Use Policy

Employees must:

  • Use company email for business purposes only
  • Report security incidents immediately
  • Follow all IT security procedures
  • Maintain confidentiality of company information
